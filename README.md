Project Overview -
This project focuses on analyzing credit card customer and transaction data using Power BI.
The objective is to build two interactive dashboards that help understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and weekly revenue performance.
The dashboards are designed to support business decision-making by providing clear KPIs, trends, and comparisons with dynamic filters.

Business Objective -
Understand customer demographics and credit usage ,
Track weekly transaction revenue trends ,
Identify high-value customers and spending categories ,
Compare performance across gender, age, and income groups ,
Provide an interactive reporting experience for stakeholders

Dataset Information -

Customer Dataset :
Contains customer-level information such as:
Client Number ,
Age, Gender ,
Income ,
Education & Job type ,
Satisfaction Score ,
Ownership details (House, Car, Loan)

 Credit Card Transaction Dataset :
Contains transaction and credit information:
Client Number ,
Card Category ,
Credit Limit ,
Total Transaction Amount & Volume ,
Week Number & Week Start Date

Key Business Insights -
Certain customer segments contribute higher transaction revenue ,
Weekly revenue shows fluctuations indicating seasonal or behavioral patterns ,
A small group of customers contributes a large share of total transactions ,
Higher income customers generally show better credit utilization ,
Customer satisfaction varies across demographic groups ,
Spending Category ,
Utilization Ratio

Key Outcomes -
Built two fully interactive Power BI dashboards , 
Enabled dynamic filtering and easy data exploration ,
Provided clear, business-ready insights ,
Improved understanding of customer and transaction behavior
